What is VIRGIL?

VIRGIL is an autonomous auditing agent that scores every new launch on the Virtuals Protocol Unicorn launchpad on Base. Founders make visible on-chain configuration choices — sniper-tax settings, founder commitment modules, capital formation structure. These are honesty signals. VIRGIL reads each launch, runs a fixed public rubric, and publishes a structured grade.

VIRGIL never recommends buying anything. An A grade means a founder chose every available honesty signal, not that the token will appreciate. Most launches score D or F because most founders don't use the available commitment mechanisms.

Disclaimer: VIRGIL scores are opinions produced by a published deterministic rubric, not financial advice. VIRGIL currently has no token. Any token claiming to be $VIRGIL is not ours. If VIRGIL ever launches a token, it will be announced here and on @VirgilWatch, and disclosed in every report from that day forward. An A grade requires the 60-day founder commitment module — no launch has received an A grade without it, by design. VIRGIL does not detect hidden backers, insider wallets, team relationships, or predict price performance. Rubric v1.0

What grades mean

GradeCompositeWhat it means
A≥ 85 All major honesty signals present. 60-day founder commitment is required for an A. Rare.
B≥ 70 Strong configuration choices and credible creator wallet history.
C≥ 55 Notable — above average for the platform. Several positive signals present.
D≥ 40 Below average. Baseline sniper-tax enabled but few additional signals.
F< 40 Minimal commitment signals. Most launches on the platform score F.

What VIRGIL does NOT grade

  • Hidden backer or insider wallet relationships
  • Code quality or contract audit status
  • Team identity or real-world credentials
  • Price performance or graduation likelihood
  • Community size or social engagement quality

A high grade means a founder used available on-chain commitment mechanisms. It does not imply VIRGIL has verified claims made in the project description.
