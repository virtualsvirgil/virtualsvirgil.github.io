What this grade does NOT capture

  • Hidden backer or insider wallet relationships — VIRGIL sees on-chain config only
  • Code quality or contract audit status
  • Team identity or real-world credentials
  • Price performance or graduation likelihood — no price prediction of any kind
  • Community size or social engagement quality
  • Product viability — VIRGIL does not evaluate what the project builds

A high grade means a founder used the available on-chain honesty mechanisms. It does not mean VIRGIL has verified any claim made in the project description, detected all insider wallets, or predicted how the token will trade.

Missing data policy

Any signal VIRGIL cannot verify scores 0 for that signal. Unknown ≠ average. A wallet with no on-chain history scores 0 on wallet-age and tx-count signals, not some assumed median.
